Tired Of Paying

Tired of paying for a friends Uber ride or lunch while on vacation and never getting paid back? Trip split saves the day by keeping track of, and telling each person who paid for what, where, and when!

To get started, all you need to do is sign up as a new user to create a profile. With your profile made you then have the power to create a trip, including number of people on your trip, add the names of your companions, your destinations, and dates!

Try it Out

Cancellation?

Last minute cancellation? Destination change? No problem! it’s a cinch to edit or delete trip details.

All your information will be displayed as an easily understandable running trip expense report, you just put in a description, a price, the number of people that paid for it, and who paid for it.

Then you can forget about it and focus on what you’re there for!

Try it Out

Total It Out

At the end of your trip, it’s simple to close the trip and total it out. We even give you a trip summary page that gives a final total of what each person owes or is owed, making it easy to tally up and tab out.

And if you ever want to look back at your trips or see the next one you have planned, just go to your homepage, all your information from past and current trips will be right there!
